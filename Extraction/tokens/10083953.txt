The

psychiatric

the

This

briefly

to
refuse
psychiatric
treatment
especially
medication
Emphasis

placed
on
the
clinical
issues
including
the

and
the
management
of
treatment
refusal
Empirical
data
are
provided
to
illustrate
the
rights
driven
and
treatment
driven
models
of
involuntary
psychotropic
medication
administration
