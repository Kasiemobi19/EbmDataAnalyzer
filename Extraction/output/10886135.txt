The British journal of dermatology

Article Title 
Guidelines for the management of tinea capitis. British Association of Dermatologists.
Abstract Text 
These guidelines for the management of tinea capitis have been prepared for dermatologists on behalf of the British Association of Dermatologists.
They present evidence-based guidance for treatment, with identification of the strength of evidence available at the time of preparation of the guidelines, and a brief overview of epidemiological aspects, diagnosis and investigation.
