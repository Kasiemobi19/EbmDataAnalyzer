Title 
Journal of the American Academy of Child and Adolescent Psychiatry

Article Title 
Practice parameter for the use of stimulant medications in the treatment of children, adolescents, and adults.
Abstract Text 
This practice parameter describes treatment with stimulant medication.
It uses an evidence-based medicine approach derived from a detailed literature review and expert consultation.
Stimulant medications in clinical use include methylphenidate, dextroamphetamine, mixed-salts amphetamine, and pemoline.
It carries FDA indications for treatment of attention-deficit/hyperactivity disorder and narcolepsy.
